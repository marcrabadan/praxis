# Discovery Report — <Feature title>

> Step 1 of `feature-development`. Produced before any spec exists. This is where
> understanding happens — no solutions yet. Routed to the **business-analyst**.
> Gate 1 (`approved-discovery`) accepts discovery **and** research together
> before the spec step opens.

## Problem statement

The problem being solved, in the user's terms. Not a solution.

## Business goals

The outcomes the business expects. Tie to a metric where possible.

## Stakeholders

Who is affected and who decides. (Consider a RACI if non-trivial.)

## Constraints

Technical, business, legal, and time constraints discovered so far.

## Assumptions

Things believed true that still need validation. Each becomes a research target
or an open question.

## Risks

Known risks at this stage.

## Open questions

Unresolved questions. An open question that gates the next step is a **stop
condition** — resolve it before advancing (see `rules/stop-conditions.md`).
