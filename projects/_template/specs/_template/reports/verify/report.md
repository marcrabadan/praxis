# Verify report

> Authorized once tasks are done and test evidence exists (the `verify` gate).
> This is the evidence the work meets the spec.

## What was verified

The behavior checked against the spec's acceptance criteria.

## Evidence

Test runs, commands, outputs (or links to CI). State plainly what passed, what
was skipped, and what remains unverified.

## Verdict

`meets-spec` / `gaps-found` — and the follow-ups if any.
