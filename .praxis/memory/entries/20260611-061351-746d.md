Companion to 20260611-061334-c72d. Before this change, the top hotspots
were CHANGELOG.md (x8), and the three integrations/.../software-architect.*
generated bundles (x5 each) — all structurally touched by nearly every
cross-cutting change (CHANGELOG.md by convention; integrations/** and
SKILLS.md regenerated by 'make integrations' / 'make catalog' from
.claude/skills + .claude/commands). These will always top the list and don't
indicate accumulated complexity in any one area, burying genuine hotspots.

Decision: scan_touched_files() in tools/patterns.py excludes CHANGELOG.md and
SKILLS.md (exact match) and any path under integrations/ (prefix match) from
the hotspot signal. Documented in the 'Systemic complexity' subsection of
software-architect/references/practices.md and in tools/patterns.py's module
docstring.
