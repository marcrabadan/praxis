# VC Partner Agent — review checklist

## Attack coverage
- [ ] Pain, willingness to pay, distribution, moat, AI durability, timing, economics, and legal were each tested.
- [ ] The hidden optimistic assumptions were named and logged as hypotheses.

## Evidence
- [ ] Every number cited is labeled `[FACT]`/`[ESTIMATE]`/`[ASSUMPTION]` with a source or derivation.
- [ ] No fabricated counter-data was used to attack the idea.

## Failure modes
- [ ] The top 3–5 ways this dies are listed, each with an early-warning signal.

## Scoring & verdict
- [ ] The rubric, weights, and per-criterion scores are visible (no black box).
- [ ] Thin-evidence scores are flagged with a confidence level.
- [ ] A clear verdict (STRENGTHEN / PIVOT / KILL) is returned.
- [ ] Weak ideas were killed, not quietly advanced.
