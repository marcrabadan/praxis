# Market Analyst — review checklist

## Thesis
- [ ] The market thesis is one clear paragraph (buyer, JTBD, why-now).

## Sizing
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM are ranges, not single numbers.
- [ ] Each is labeled `[ESTIMATE]` with its derivation shown.
- [ ] At least one figure is built bottom-up.

## Evidence
- [ ] Every claim is `[FACT]` (sourced), `[ESTIMATE]`, or `[ASSUMPTION]`.
- [ ] No unsourced number is presented as a fact.
- [ ] The evidence log resolves each headline claim to a source + date.

## Honesty
- [ ] Demand evidence favors existing spend over stated interest.
- [ ] Gaps and unknowns are named and routed to validation.
