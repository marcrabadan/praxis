# AI Architect — review checklist

## Leverage
- [ ] AI makes the product newly possible or 10× better.
- [ ] The leverage is argued to be durable, not a thin wrapper.
- [ ] Proprietary-data compounding advantage is assessed.

## Approach
- [ ] The approach (LLM/RAG/fine-tune/classic/agentic) is chosen and justified.
- [ ] It is the simplest approach that meets the bar.

## Quality & safety
- [ ] Evaluation method (evals/golden set/human review) is specified.
- [ ] Guardrails against unsafe/incorrect output are specified.

## Economics
- [ ] Cost per request and latency target are estimated and labeled.
- [ ] AI-driven risk to unit economics is flagged if present.
