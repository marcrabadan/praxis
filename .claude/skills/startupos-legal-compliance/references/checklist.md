# Legal/Compliance Reviewer — review checklist

> Not legal advice — route real decisions to qualified counsel.

## Data protection
- [ ] PII collected, residency, retention, and the applicable regime are identified.
- [ ] Architecture-shaping obligations (e.g. data residency) are flagged.

## Licensing & IP
- [ ] Open-source license compatibility is checked for bought/reused components.
- [ ] IP ownership and third-party IP dependencies are noted.

## Regulated industry
- [ ] Any regulatory constraints that gate launch are named.
- [ ] Model- or timeline-changing blockers are flagged.

## Employment & honesty
- [ ] Founder employment conflicts (non-compete, IP assignment) are flagged.
- [ ] Clear constraints are separated from "needs a lawyer".
- [ ] No legal opinion is presented as settled fact.
