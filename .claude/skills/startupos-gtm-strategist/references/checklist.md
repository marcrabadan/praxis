# GTM Strategist — review checklist

## Beachhead & positioning
- [ ] The beachhead is narrow and reachable.
- [ ] Positioning names a category, a message, and the switch from status quo.

## Motion & channels
- [ ] The motion fits the buyer and the price point.
- [ ] Each channel has a CAC hypothesis (`[ESTIMATE]`) and a test.

## First cohort
- [ ] There is a concrete, non-scalable first-100-customers plan with real places to look.

## Funnel & risk
- [ ] Funnel metrics have targets, labeled as estimates.
- [ ] The expansion path is stated.
- [ ] GTM risks (channel saturation, CAC blow-out, long cycles) have early-warning signals.
