# CEO Agent — review checklist

Run before facilitating a gate or returning a consolidated narrative.

## Thesis
- [ ] The thesis is stated in one sentence and is coherent end to end.
- [ ] It traces to the evidence the other agents produced.

## Honesty
- [ ] Facts, assumptions, estimates, and hypotheses are separated.
- [ ] No assumption is presented as a fact.
- [ ] The strongest disconfirming evidence is included, not buried.

## Gate facilitation
- [ ] Recommendation, Evidence, Risks, and Alternatives are all present.
- [ ] The human is given a clear choice, not steered to one answer.
- [ ] The open assumptions the human would accept are named.
- [ ] The decision (approver, date, rationale) is recorded in memory.

## Discipline
- [ ] Weak ideas were killed or sent back, not carried for comfort.
- [ ] No selection or export was self-approved — a human approved each.
