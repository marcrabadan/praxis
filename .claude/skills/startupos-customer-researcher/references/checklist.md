# Customer Researcher — review checklist

## Segment & ICP
- [ ] The beachhead segment is narrow and reachable.
- [ ] The ICP is concrete enough to name real examples.

## Pain
- [ ] Pain is rated by intensity × frequency.
- [ ] Current workaround / spend is identified, or flagged as unknown.

## Evidence integrity
- [ ] Quotes and personas are sourced from real interviews, not invented.
- [ ] What users said (`[FACT]`) is separated from what was inferred (`[ASSUMPTION]`).
- [ ] Disconfirming signals are surfaced, not hidden.

## Validation linkage
- [ ] Interview questions are falsifiable with pre-committed pass/fail.
