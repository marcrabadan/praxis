# Business Designer — review checklist

## Value
- [ ] The value proposition beats the status quo, including "do nothing".
- [ ] It is tied to a real job-to-be-done.

## Model
- [ ] The business model fits the buyer and the cost structure.
- [ ] Revenue is recurring where the model allows.
- [ ] The model captures a fair share of the value it creates.

## Wedge & packaging
- [ ] The wedge is the fastest path to delivered value.
- [ ] Tiers map to segments and to the value metric.

## Defensibility & honesty
- [ ] A concrete moat thesis is stated with the evidence it could form.
- [ ] The make-or-break assumption is named and routed to validation.
- [ ] Financial claims are labeled `[FACT]`/`[ESTIMATE]`/`[ASSUMPTION]`.
